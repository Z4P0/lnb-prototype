Personal Checking Services

Checking for everyone so they can do just about everything

We’ve designed our personal checking programs with one simple goal in mind: so that our customers have a checking solution that’s perfect for them. Of course, every offering comes with LNB’s exceptional service and convenient technology.

Prefer no-frills checking?

Avoid high minimum-balance requirements and maintenance fees, and don’t pay for features and services you don’t need.

  • $25 minimum opening deposit
  • No minimum monthly balance
  • First 8 debits each month are free; only $0.25/debit after that (withdrawals, check paid, automatic transfers or payment)
Looking for convenience?

Smart, convenient, straightforward—there’s nothing regular about this checking plan.

  • $25 minimum opening deposit
  • No monthly maintenance fees and no minimum balance requirements when you have direct deposit, are 55 years of age* or older, or if your balance remains at $300 or more during the month
  • Unlimited transactions
* Primary account holder
Want to grow your account faster?

Earn interest on your balance while it’s in your NOW interest checking account.

  • $1,000 minimum opening deposit
  • Earn a competitive annual percentage yield (APY) on checking account balances of $1,000 or more
  • Interest accrues daily and is paid monthly
  • No maintenance fee on daily balances of $1,000 or more
Want to save and grow healthcare funds?

Pay for current health expenses, and save for future qualified medical expenses and retiree health expenses –all on a tax-free basis.

  • Tiered interest is accrued daily and paid monthly
  • Tiered interest earnings for bigger savings
  • No monthly maintenance fee or minimum balance requirement
  • No per-debit fees
  • Access your funds in branch, by check, or as an ATM/POS transaction
